OUR SOLUTION

Like M-PESA or MTN Mobile Money did for financial services, or like IBM innovated the infrastructure that propelled the personal computing industry, we are bringing quality healthcare closer to people's homes with micro-clinics operated by medical professionals who are empowered with AI and portable diagnostic tools to provide primary care and secondary prevention of disease.

Our approach is simple

MaryHealth micro-clinics are modular and can be transported to any parts of the world. They provide the physical space for patients to interact with care providers. Care providers have access to our software, AI models, medical equipment, supplies, and the technology needed to provide primary care and secondary prevention of diseases.
