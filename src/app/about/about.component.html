PROBLEM

Imagine a world where a single doctor is responsible for the care of 60,000 patients. This is the harsh reality in some African nations, where a lack of access to affordable, quality healthcare is a major problem. With 9.2 million deaths attributed to communicable and non-communicable diseases, the need for early diagnosis and treatment is vital. However, the shortage of Radiologists in Africa is a major obstacle in achieving this goal. This is a crisis that needs urgent attention.
